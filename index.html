Who We Are

About the Trust

The Kasireddy Narasimha Reddy Charitable Trust is a public charitable organization established to promote the spiritual, physical, mental, and educational development of all people — without distinction of caste, creed, or religion.

We promote welfare, social, cultural, charitable, health, education, humanitarian, and relief activities that protect, safeguard, and improve the quality of human life — particularly for economically weaker sections of society — so that they may lead healthy, happy, contented, and dignified lives.

Our Foundation

Aims & Objectives

The Trust is organised for the promotion and upliftment of adults and children of all ages, irrespective of caste, creed, race, colour, sex, or religion, in the interest of mankind in general.

01

Welfare & Humanitarian Activities

Promote welfare, social, cultural, charitable, health, education, humanitarian, and relief activities that protect, safeguard, and raise the standard of human life, particularly for economically weaker sections of society.

02

Medical & Rehabilitation Centers

Help and collaborate in establishing and running medical centers, old age homes, ashrams, rehabilitation institutions for the destitute, vocational training centers for the handicapped, and self job-oriented activities for the unemployed.

03

Scholarships & Higher Education

Offer scholarships, stipends, and financial support to deserving students pursuing higher studies in Medicine, Arts, Science, Engineering, and Computer Technology.

04

Peace, Tolerance & National Integration

Undertake and support programs that provide educational, recreational, social, cultural, and spiritual activities with the sole purpose of promoting peace, love, tolerance, and national integration among various communities and nationalities.

05

Sponsorship of Institutional Care

Sponsorship of economically weak persons for institutional care, ensuring the most vulnerable members of society receive the support and dignity they deserve.

06

Collaboration with Voluntary Organizations

Collaborate with other voluntary organizations, NGOs, and social groups to collectively accomplish the stated objects and extend the reach of the Trust's mission.

07

Affiliation with National & International Bodies

Affiliate with national and international organizations engaged in welfare, health education, national integration, and peace-promotion activities to strengthen the Trust's global impact.

08

Government & NGO Dialogue

Maintain regular dialogue and rapport with Central and State Governments and NGOs to elicit support and cooperation in fulfilling the aims and objects of the Trust.

09

Acquisition of Property

Acquire immovable and movable properties by purchase, lease, mortgage, gifts, grants, legacy, bequest, or exchange from any person, company, society, government, or cooperative body to further the objectives of the Trust.

10

Construction & Infrastructure

Erect and build residential complexes, hostels, hospitals, and other structures to further the objects of the Trust, and maintain, manage, control, improve, alter, repair, or reconstruct the same as needed.
