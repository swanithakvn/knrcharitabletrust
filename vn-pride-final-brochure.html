What is V N Pride?
ఏమిటి ఈ వి యన్ ప్రైడ్?

V N Pride is a massive DTCP-approved Integrated Gated Community Township spanning 25 acres of land. In its first phase, 22 acres are DTCP approved and divided into 231 thoughtfully planned house plots. All required tests for building construction, including Soil Test and Water Test, were completed before applying for DTCP approval, which has now been finalized.

Where is V N Pride?
ఈ వి యన్ ప్రైడ్ ఎక్కడ ఉంది?

V N Pride is strategically located on Highway No 716, Rajampet - Tirupathi Road, just 60 minutes from Temple City Tirupathi and 10 minutes from Rajampet.

Infrastructure Details

M20 Concrete Roads: 7 inches thick with 1:1.5:3 ratio. All internal roads are 24 feet wide within 40-foot streets.

Underground Utilities: UPVC drainage with SN4 standard. Underground electricity for safety.

Water Supply: 1.2 lakh liters overhead tank. 6 Kg/cm² rated pipes for direct supply to third floor.

Compound Wall: 8-foot wall with power fencing. Interlocking bricks (6-inch). 50-year lifetime.

Parks: 2.2 acres total. Central park (1 acre) with games, outdoor gym, and washrooms. Sports park with all facilities.

Avenue Plantation: Medicinal trees (Mango, Guava, Jackfruit, Neem, Bilwa, Arjuna, Anjeera) along footpaths.
